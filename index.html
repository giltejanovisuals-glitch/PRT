About Me

I create visuals where creativity meets commercial purpose.

I’m Gil Tejano, a Creative Specialist and Graphic Designer focused on creating polished, brand-consistent visuals for furniture, lighting, retail, lifestyle, and manufacturing brands.

My work covers product campaigns, catalogs, social media design, photo manipulation, retail displays, video, 3D visuals, and digital interfaces. I approach every project with the goal of making the brand clear, purposeful, and visually memorable.
