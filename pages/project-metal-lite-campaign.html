Case Study

01

The Challenge

The launch needed to look consistent everywhere it appeared — in-store, in print, and on social — despite each channel demanding a completely different format, size, and viewing distance, from a shelf talker read in seconds to a banner ad scaled down to a phone screen.

02

The Creative Approach

I built one shared key visual, then adapted it into a strict system of retail signage, print collateral, and digital ad variants — so every touchpoint read as the same campaign instead of a collection of one-off assets designed in isolation from each other.

03

The Contribution

I led the campaign from concept through final retail-ready files — art directing the key visual, building the layout system, and producing every signage, print, and digital variant so the in-house team could roll it out without redesigning a single touchpoint.
